Self Help

Explore self-help techniques and take assessments to understand yourself better.

Useful Techniques

  • Academic Performance: Create a timetable, identify areas for improvement, and seek help from classmates or faculty.
  • Goal Orientation: Break goals into small, daily achievable tasks. Take one step at a time.
  • Stress Management: Take a gadget break, talk to family or friends, go for a walk, listen to music, or reach out to a counselor.
  • Grounding Technique: Use grounding to stay present and manage overwhelming emotions.
